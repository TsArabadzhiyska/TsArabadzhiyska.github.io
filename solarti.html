About Solarti

Solarti is a brand of CreativeArt , a high school company manufactures these amazing street number plates made of porcelain, clay and other pieces of mosaics. Some have solar panels and they glow in the dark - showing the way to your home! Imagine: in thousands of years this is what will speak of you to the archaeologists from the future...
