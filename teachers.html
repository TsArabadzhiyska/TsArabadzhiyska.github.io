ATANAS KAMESHEV

Atanas Kameshev was born on March 28, 1965 in Plovdiv. 1984 graduated High School of Art "Tsanko Lavrenov" in Plovdiv 1996 Graduated National University of Arts - Mural Painting 1996 He participated in painting of the church "St. Archangel Michael" inNessebar . 2002 He made a solo exhibition of Orthodox mural paintings in Poznan, Poland 2004 He made a solo exhibition of Orthodox mural paintings in Island Vielkopolski, Poland. 2008 participated in the painting of iconostasis in the church "St. Sofia" inChicago
