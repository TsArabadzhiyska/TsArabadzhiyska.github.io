HISTORY OF MAA

The Mural Art Academy is created by National Art High School "Tsanko Lavrenov" in 1975. Since thet the speciality has been developing more and more every year.The main Teacher Atanas Kameshev was at the 3-rd graduated class to this speciality. The second teacher Kristina Kutlova has graduated from the same speciality. They have obtained university degrees at the National Academy of Arts.

Basic Education Information

The students of the fresco specialty are trained for the realization of projects related to a certain architectural environment and scale, preparation of cardboard and realization in material. In the process of learning, basic mural techniques are used - sgraffito, cedar, mosaic and stained glass.Mosaics and natural stone PROCESSED GLASS decorate private homes ,Stained glass for private homes, villas .
