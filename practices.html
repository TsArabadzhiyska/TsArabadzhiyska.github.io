ABOUT OUR SUMMER PRACTICES

The best way to train your mural art knowledges is tho do it in the real life situations. That's why every year our academy offers our students to join the summer two weeks long practice.There they acquire a team work skills.They can choose between working with mosaic team or painting on a wall with a fresco team.Also they have the opportunity to join a beach painting outdoor.
